Shiosai no Majo chapter 6

Shiosai no Majo is a short manga series about a witch who runs a restaurant where customers can eat food made from their troubles. Each chapter sees a new character visit the witch and come to understand their worries by eating her food.

Chapter 6 is a prolonged flashback of a customer who was overcome by feelings of inferiority. This section of the script is the witch talking to the customer about their troubles.
